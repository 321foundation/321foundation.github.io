An in-classroom, teacher-led program focused on achieving foundational literacy & numeracy skills for students of grades 1 to 5.

WHAT IS PARaM?

PARaM stands on the four pillars of Prepare, Assess, Remediate and Motivate - to deliver student learning outcomes.


Our Impact on Learning Outcomes

WHAT IS PARaM?

We prepare teachers to be experts in teaching language and Maths

We assess students & teachers' progression to chart the best learning path for both

We provide customised support to teachers to remediate learning gaps in students

We motivate teachers & students by celebrating both efforts made and goals met

Our Impact on Learning Outcomes



WHO BENEFITS FROM PARaM?

Students achieve their learning goals and get a fair chance at higher studies.

Teachers get an opportunity to upskill and become proficient.

School leaders get expert professional support at a very low cost.

Parents get to see the benefits of foundational learning for their children.
