WHAT IS PARaM?

PARaM stands on the four pillars of Prepare, Assess, Remediate and Motivate - to deliver student learning outcomes.


Our Impact on Learning Outcomes

School-level M&E dashboard to empower leaders’ decision making

Customised lesson plans to augment the school's existing curriculum

Assessment systems, tools and processes to gauge students’ learning progress

Support with parent engagement to help schools shine in their local communities

Support with teacher attrition management to sustain changes in events of sudden changes in school personnel

Institutionalising the FLN captain position to supplement the leaders’ responsibilities

THE GOLDEN BIRD



At one point India was known as the Golden Bird, which symbolised pride, prosperity, and glory. We exist to make sure that that glory is not just in our past but also our future.

That we not only share the stories about our glorious past but show them the greatness of Modern India. We exist to catalyze India's re-emergence as the golden bird.
