A classroom with 321's intervention in 2019-20 was likely to triple the number of children who can read with understanding and correctly solve basic Maths problems.

Our current program i-Ignite helps our students achieve FLN outcomes from the safety of their home.

That we not only share the stories about our glorious past but show them the greatness of Modern India. We exist to catalyze India's re-emergence as the golden bird.
