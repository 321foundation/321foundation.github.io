OUR JOURNEY

Since 2012, we have delivered impactful, user-focused and data-driven programs to educators to ensure quality education for the most vulnerable and marginalized students. Our current aim is to ensure the attainment of foundational learning outcomes for over half a million children by 2025.

2020-present

FLN program is pivoted to address challenges faced by schools due to COVID-19 related closures. Once children steadily and safely get back to classroom, our current program PARaM aims to bridge the learning losses and sustain foundational learning levels to ensure that our children have a fair chance at higher studies and future life-opportunities.

2018-20

Foundational literacy and numeracy (FLN) program is piloted and scaled to 8000 students that tripled the number of children who can read with understanding and correctly solve basic Maths problems.

2016-18

Capacity-building program for teachers expands to three cities - Mumbai, Hyderabad and Bangalore impacting 2,500 teachers.

2014-16

321 school becomes a part of Akanksha. Drawing on the lessons learnt in the school, teacher-training pilots begin in few schools in Mumbai and Hyderabad.

2012-14

321 school starts operations at Masjid Bunder Mumbai with 60 students and expands to 200 students by year 2.

OUR INITIATIVES

Bharat EdTech Initiative

2022 - present

We are community partners of a nation-wide initiative that aims to enhance learning outcomes for first-generation digital learners by leveraging at-home learning time through proven EdTech solutions.

FLN program: PARaM

2021 - present

We are directly impacting the learning outcomes of 18000 students in 450 classrooms across 6 cities in India. We do this by building the capacity of 500 teachers through a hybrid delivery model.

Online resource banks: CAPE & E-Shaala

2020 - 2021

We helped 15000 teachers in 1000 schools across the under-resourced communities of India with ready-to-use lessons to improve at-home learning for 300000 students during COVID-19-related school closures.

Collaboration with Govt. of NCT of Delhi

2020 - 2021

We trained teachers in the core academic unit to enhance content creation process and facilitated content distribution for 12 lakh students. We also conducted workshops over 6 days for trainers from DIET, SCERT to improve the facilitation skills of the participants.

FLN Programs: Ignite 3.0 & i-Ignite

2018 - 2021

Tripled the % of students achieving grade level outcomes in numeracy and literacy in 72 schools across three cities. We could also help the same schools sustain these learning gains through the pandemic.

Capacity Building Programs: Ignite 1.0 & 2.0

2016 - 2019

Improved teaching-learning practices of 2500 teachers across 80 schools in Mumbai, Hyderabad & Bengaluru. An independent evaluation showed that there was 18% growth in teachers performance between 2017-2019.

3.2.1 School

2012 - 2016

Holistically improved learning outcomes for 200 students in Mumbai. The learnings and best practices derived at school laid the foundations for all our future programs. The school was then transitioned to the Akanksha Foundation.

THE GOLDEN BIRD



At one point India was known as the Golden Bird, which symbolised pride, prosperity, and glory. We exist to make sure that that glory is not just in our past but also our future.

That we not only share the stories about our glorious past but show them the greatness of Modern India. We exist to catalyze India's re-emergence as the golden bird.
