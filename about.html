OUR JOURNEY

We started our journey in 2012 by opening a school for children from low-income communities in Mumbai. Our experience in running the school led us to understand the impact of high quality teaching on a child's learning. We have since focused on building customized programs to empower educators.

WHO WE ARE

We are a young, energetic, curious, innovative team that cares deeply about the state of education in India.

THE GOLDEN BIRD



At one point India was known as the Golden Bird, which symbolised pride, prosperity, and glory. We exist to make sure that that glory is not just in our past but also our future.

That we not only share the stories about our glorious past but show them the greatness of Modern India. We exist to catalyze India's re-emergence as the golden bird.
