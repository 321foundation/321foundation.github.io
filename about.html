OUR JOURNEY

Since 2012, we have delivered impactful, user-focused and data-driven programs to educators to ensure quality education for the most vulnerable and marginalized students. Our current aim is to ensure the attainment of foundational learning outcomes for over half a million children by 2025.

2020-present

FLN program is pivoted to address challenges faced by schools due to COVID-19 related closures. Once children steadily and safely get back to classroom, our current program PARaM aims to bridge the learning losses and sustain foundational learning levels to ensure that our children have a fair chance at higher studies and future life-opportunities.

2018-20

Foundational literacy and numeracy (FLN) program is piloted and scaled to 8000 students that tripled the number of children who can read with understanding and correctly solve basic Maths problems.

2016-18

Capacity-building program for teachers expands to three cities - Mumbai, Hyderabad and Bangalore impacting 2,500 teachers.

2014-16

321 school becomes a part of Akanksha. Drawing on the lessons learnt in the school, teacher-training pilots begin in few schools in Mumbai and Hyderabad.

2012-14

321 school starts operations at Masjid Bunder Mumbai with 60 students and expands to 200 students by year 2.

THE GOLDEN BIRD

That we not only share the stories about our glorious past but show them the greatness of Modern India. We exist to catalyze India's re-emergence as the golden bird.
