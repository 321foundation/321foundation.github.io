WHO IS E-SHAALA FOR?

E-Shaala can be used by all educators

Teachers who need resources to teach online and whose students have devices or data issues.

School leaders who want to engage students and cater to parents' needs in a simple, low-cost way.

Parents whose aim is to ensure their children continue to learn from the safety of their homes.

THE GOLDEN BIRD



At one point India was known as the Golden Bird, which symbolised pride, prosperity, and glory. We exist to make sure that that glory is not just in our past but also our future.

That we not only share the stories about our glorious past but show them the greatness of Modern India. We exist to catalyze India's re-emergence as the golden bird.
