Building a strong foundation for children


Around 130 million school-going children in India study in primary grades. But, 7 out of 10 children have not attained the foundational literacy and numeracy (FLN) skills required for success in higher studies. As a result, they cannot advance as productive citizens of the country.

We aim to ensure the attainment of foundational learning outcomes for over half a million children by 2025.

That we not only share the stories about our glorious past but show them the greatness of Modern India. We exist to catalyze India's re-emergence as the golden bird.
